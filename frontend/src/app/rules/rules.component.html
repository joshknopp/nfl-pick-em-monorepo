How to Play

  • Buy-in is $50 via Venmo or PayPal.
  • $50 goes to the house, $50 for second place, and the winner takes the rest.
  • Submit or change picks any time up until the scheduled kickoff time.
  • Picks are submitted as soon as you click the team button. There is no separate Submit button.
  • NEW: Failure to submit a pick for a game is an automatic loss. It is a good idea to pick all games well in advance, and then change as needed.
  • Any game that ends in a tie counts as a loss for everybody.
  • Login methods are Google and email/password. Google is better. If you use email and forget your password, you have to request a reset and then wait a while for a site admin to process it. Don't be that guy!
